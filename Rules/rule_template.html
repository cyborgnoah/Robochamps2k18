Ricochet - CS GO

General Rules

  1. The names of both the captain and the team should be given to the GC members 5 min before the starting of the match.
  2. There‟ll be a veto process for Bo1 among 7 maps: DUST2, MIRAGE, CACHE, OVERPASS, COBBLESTONE, INFERNO, TRAIN. Only the captains will join the server for veto map process.
  3. There will be a knife round among the 2 captains to decide who will decide first. The winner gets to decide if he wants to go first or second.
  4. Turn by turn each captain will remove the map his team doesn‟t want to play.
  5. At the end, the person who gets to select among remaining 2 maps will decide which map his team wants to play. Note: Among the remaining 2 maps, the player will actually select the map they want to play. It will not be a veto.
  6. The chosen map will be played between the 2 teams.
  7. In case of any doubts, you will be guided at the time of veto process. So don‟t worry much about this process right now
  8. After the veto process, all the members will join the server and can configure their sensitivity, crosshair, video and graphics settings, etc
  9. There will be a warm up time of exactly 5 minutes for both the teams which will be done on a death match server on the selected map which will be played.
  10. Increasing warm up time will not be encouraged at all cost, so the participants are required to not request for the same

Match Rules

  1. The players are expected to remember these simple commands which are to be typed in the main chat:
    • /r or /ready or /rdy - Marks you ready
    • /ur or /nr or /unready or /notready - Marks you not ready
    • /s or /score - Displays score if live
    • /pause - To pause the match (Will pause at the end of round)
    • /unpause - To unpause the match
  2. /stay & /switch – Stay or switch side after knife round
  3. When players have marked as ready, knife round will start which will decide the sides.
  4. The captain of the winner team will decide the side his team wants to play by typing /stay or /switch in the main chat.
  5. The match will go LIVE next and will be played according to the competitive mode in CS:GO.
  6. In case the team wants to pause, they can type /pause in main chat which will pause the match at the end of the round. Similarly, they can un-pause by typing /unpause.
  7. Note: Only 2 pauses per team per half will be given. The pause duration will not be more than 2 minutes.
  8. In case of a draw, overtime will be played in which 10000 dollars will be given to each team and 6 rounds will be played, 3 each side and the team to clinch 4 rounds will be winner. If not, overtime will be played until the winner is decided.

Ricochet - NFS Most Wanted

General Rules

  1. Match Will be 1v1
  2. The player who reaches the finish line first will be declared the winner.
  3. In case of a tie or some other technical issue (game crashing), the race is not completed then both the players will have to play on the same track with the same cars as that of previous race.
  4. The set of cars and playable tracks will be told to you beforehand and in no circumstances can these be changed unless there is a very legit issue.
  5. In case of multiple races among the same players, a 5 min break will be given solely for the purpose of selecting a car/colour for the race from the given pool of cars.
  6. All mods will be disabled on every race to allow an even playing field.
  7. In case of multiple races between two players, the player who wins the higher no races is to be declared the winner, however, if both players
  8. tie by winning equal number of races (in case of even no of races) then the player with the higher number of points is declared the winner.
